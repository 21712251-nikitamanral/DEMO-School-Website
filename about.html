About Our School

Welcome to G.I.C Patelnagar

G.I.C Patelnagar is a place of learning, inspiration, and opportunity. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, confidence, and compassion to become future leaders. The school fosters a friendly, inclusive environment where academics, creativity, and personal growth thrive hand in hand.

Located in a serene environment, the school features modern classrooms, technology-enabled labs, a rich library, and lush green play areas. We are committed to excellence in education and believe in nurturing both academic and emotional intelligence.

Why Choose Us?

Experienced Faculty

Dedicated teachers who guide, inspire, and nurture students every step of the way.

Modern Infrastructure

Smart classrooms, science and computer labs, library, and well-maintained playgrounds.

Holistic Development

Balanced focus on academics, sports, arts, and values for well-rounded growth.
